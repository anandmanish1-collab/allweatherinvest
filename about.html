About AM Wealth Advisors

I’m YOUR financial adviser — Anand Manish, registered with SEBI (Investment Advisor INA000xxxx).

Experience

My financial career began trading futures on rates and currencies. I witnessed the 2008 bond and currency dislocations first-hand and later served at the Employees' Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) for a decade. Over time my approach evolved from speculation to disciplined portfolio management emphasizing equities and bonds.

Philosophy

Long-term, diversified equity exposure is a proven inflation hedge. We prefer rule-based conviction over tips or short-lived themes. Our custom portfolios are built from two inputs: your risk profile and the market conditions at the time of onboarding.

  • Portfolio restructuring & rebalancing
  • Tax-aware execution & reporting
  • Custom objective-based financial planning
