Frequently Asked Questions

How do I onboard?

We collect KYC, PAN, risk profile & link your broker/custodian for reporting.

What is the fee structure?

50% of advisory fees due upfront; balance after six months. GST @ 18% extra. Details are in the Compliance page.

How often do you rebalance?

Rebalances are event-driven and periodic (quarterly or as needed). Free rebalance allowances are described in our terms.
