How We Work

  1. Onboarding: KYC, risk profile, goals & custodial link ups.
  2. Plan: Asset allocation, sample portfolio & tax modelling.
  3. Invest: Execution with tax efficiency and low churn.
  4. Monitor: Quantitative monitoring and event-driven rebalances.
  5. Report: Monthly / quarterly statements & annual review.

Portfolio Construction Rules

  • Portfolio size: 25–40 stocks (core 15–20)
  • Single stock weight ≤12%; single sector ≤33%
  • Debt allocation: 5%–25% per risk profile
